Project Description – Edu Planner

Edu Planner is a smart time management and study productivity platform designed to help students stay focused, organized, and productive while studying.

The platform addresses one of the biggest challenges students face today: digital distractions, poor time management, and lack of study organization.

Edu Planner provides an interactive and student-friendly environment that combines study planning, distraction control, productivity tracking, and motivation systems in one integrated platform.

Main Features of Edu Planner

1. Smart Study Timer
A customizable study timer based on the Pomodoro Technique, helping students manage study sessions effectively and improve concentration.

2. Distraction Blocking System
The platform temporarily blocks distracting websites and notifications during study sessions to create a distraction-free environment and improve focus.

3. Task Management System
Students can create, organize, prioritize, and complete study tasks with deadlines to improve planning and reduce procrastination.

4. Progress Tracking Dashboard
Users can monitor their study performance through:
* Study hours tracking
* Daily and weekly reports
* Productivity statistics
* Performance charts

5. Gamification System
To increase motivation, Edu Planner includes:
* Points and rewards
* Achievement badges
* Study streaks
* Levels and milestones

This system makes studying more engaging and enjoyable.

6. Smart Notifications & Reminders
Automated reminders help students stay on track by notifying them about:
* Upcoming study sessions
* Task deadlines
* Daily study goals

7. Focus Mode
A dedicated study mode that minimizes distractions and encourages deep concentration during study sessions.

8. User Profile & Personalization
Each user has a personalized profile containing:
* Study progress
* Achievements
* Productivity level
* Personal study preferences

Business Strategy

The platform will be completely free for the first six months to attract users and build trust. After the growth phase, a freemium model will be introduced, where essential features remain free while advanced tools become premium.

Vision

The vision of Edu Planner is to become a leading productivity platform that helps students build discipline, improve academic performance, and develop better study habits.
